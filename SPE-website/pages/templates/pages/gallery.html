Intern Talk

  • Intern Talk
  • Ciao everyone!! We conducted our yearly marquee event, "Intern talk" on 2nd October, with the motive of guiding the bright & young minds of Petroleum Engineering on their path to excellence. With a diverse range of expertise, the final year students of the Petroleum Engineering Department shared their experiences, tips and valuable insights to crack the interview process and secure one's dream internship. The session covered the do's and don't's of an interview process, the preparation mindset, etiquettes to be followed in a G.D. round, and any possible question one can have in their mind. Kudos to all the senior year students for letting the audience get a glimpse at what it takes to earn a well-deserved internship. We are sure their instructions and advice will cater to the preparation for an internship. Till then, stay safe, stay tuned for more such events.
