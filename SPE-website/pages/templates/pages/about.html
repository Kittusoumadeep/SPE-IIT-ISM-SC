About us
Who we are?

Since 1984, SPE ISM STUDENT CHAPTER has kept expanding itself to from its being. Taking the oath to help the students, the institute and the society in general, it has been paving an incredible path. From being, one of its kind in the country to be the OUTSTANDING STUDENT CHAPTER (2010 & 2015) globally, it has not only brought laurels to the institute but also the country at the global level. Its been all possible because of its strong commitment of helping students learn more about the industry through offering the opportunity to network with company spokespeople, nurturing the exchange of ideas with others, and intensifying their knowledge through a plethora of educational and tech events throughout the year. Till date, it's in constant engagement with the students to make them global citizens by fostering the leadership as well as the management skills.
